Our Story

Born from a Purpose,
Built for People!

GO-GO Shuttles (Pty) Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to provide South Africa's corporate sector with a passenger transport partner that puts people first. We saw a gap — unreliable, unsafe, and undignified commuter transport was costing employees their time, energy, and wellbeing every single day.

We set out to change that. Today, GO-GO Shuttles operates a modern, GPS-tracked fleet across Gauteng, serving some of the country's leading corporates, institutions and event organisers. Every journey we operate is a commitment to our founding belief: that transport done right transforms lives.

As a 100% Black-owned and managed company, we are proud contributors to South Africa's transformation agenda — holding a B-BBEE Level 1 status and actively investing in employment equity, skills development, and enterprise upliftment.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, safe and reliable passenger transport that exceeds client expectations — every route, every day.

Our Vision

To be Southern Africa's most trusted, technology-driven passenger transport company — recognised for excellence and empowerment.
